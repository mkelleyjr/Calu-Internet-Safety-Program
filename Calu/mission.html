Our Mission:


The California University Internet Safety Program was designed to raise awareness of the dangers that the internet poses. The way the program works is we provide helpful tips, advice and techniques to young adults, parents and school facility on how to avoid these dangers. The goal of this program is to ultimately minimize the risk that children face growing up in a generation where the internet is more accessible than ever.
