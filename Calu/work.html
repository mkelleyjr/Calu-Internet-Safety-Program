It's Our Goal to Educate:


Now that you've gotten to know us better, we would like to extend an invitation for you. We want to make the web a safer place and we would love to teach you how. If you are interested in having us work with you, we urge your to get in contact with us!


Our plan is simple. Mr. Spencer educates children while Dr. Hsieh and Dr. Sweitzer work with parents to form a better understanding of safety on the web. Children will be engaged and the material will be suitable for and adapted towards the appropriate age levels.


If you would like to learn more or have us work with you, please don't hesitate to contact us.
